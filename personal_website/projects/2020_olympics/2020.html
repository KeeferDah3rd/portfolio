Tokyo 2020 Olympics Title

What is This Project?

This project was to make a title sequence for the 2020 Summer Olympic Games in Tokyo Japan. The second part of this project was to somehow use X-Particles in the opening. The last part of this project was to figure out a way to show a positive message and unity in the animation.

Process Deck

My Solution

For my title sequence I used X-Particles to make beams of light that would go across the city of Tokyo. The beam will all come togehter at the end to light up the Olympic logo and to show the unity of nations.

Mood and Style Board

I went the darker stlye to have more range with lighting options.

Style Frames

This phase was me playing around trying to get my style frames to look the way I wanted them to look in 3D. It also had an early version of my particle system that had many particles that go off the path that I set. The next steps for me were fix the streams of light.

Lighting

To create the lighting for the beams I used multi-passes to boost the glow effect. The streams represent the city coming alive in the excitement for the event.

Unity

The last scene represents with the beams coming together represents the unity of the Olympic Games.
