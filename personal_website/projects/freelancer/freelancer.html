What is Freelancer?

Free lancing is the way that the design industry is moving to in the next few years. The goal of my projectis to create an app that will target the growing field of self-employment. But with that many people struggle with procrastination and scheduling time. Freelancer is designed to help the user designate time to certain project while also telling them when they can take breaks. Freelancr is also assist users in running their business. This will include money management client communication.
