Standing Man (DJ Gamble Documentary)

What is Standing Man

This was the team documentary that I worked while I was participating in the RIT Kosovo Peace and Conflict Summer Program. This documentary tells the story of Adrian Berisha who is Kosovo most famous DJ. This documentary was shot in Prishtina Kosovo over the course of 3 weeks and contains footage of Adrian going about his daily life as well as preforming on stage.
