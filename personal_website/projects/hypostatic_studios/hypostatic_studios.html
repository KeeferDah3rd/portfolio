Hypostatic Studios

What is Hypostatic Studios?

Hypostatic Studios is an independent game company in Rochester New York. In the summer of 2018 I was asked to make them a bumper to introduce their game company. I was asked to use their logo as the center point and to use the theme of liquid as it pertains to their branding. To do that I made these two liquid blobs that meld together to make the Hypostatic Studios logo inside of themselves.
